Third-Party Services

MixDoctor uses the following third-party services:

Claude AI by Anthropic

We use Claude Sonnet 4.5 to generate intelligent mixing recommendations. Only anonymized technical metadata (not audio files) is sent to Anthropic's API. For more information, see Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

Apple iCloud

Optional iCloud sync uses Apple's CloudKit framework. All data synced via iCloud is protected by Apple's encryption. For more information, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

Apple App Store

Subscription payments are processed through Apple's App Store. We receive only subscription status information. For more information, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (provided by Google) to understand how users interact with our app. This helps us improve app performance, fix bugs, and enhance user experience. Firebase Analytics collects anonymized usage data such as app opens, feature usage, and crash reports. No personally identifiable information or audio content is collected. For more information, see Firebase Privacy & Security and Google's Privacy Policy.

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For users in the European Union, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You have additional rights including:

  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@mixdoctor.app.

CCPA Compliance (California Users)

For California residents, under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights
